Adama Tova; A Community-Centered Support Experience

This application was designed for the nonprofit organization Adama Tova, with the goal of creating a fast, accessible, and user-friendly platform for its community members. Adama Tova supports individuals and families affected by the events of October 7th, aiming to provide emotional support, connection, and a path toward recovery.
The app is designed to offer a clear and intuitive space where users can easily explore, register, and participate in workshops, support groups, and community-based gatherings tailored to their needs. ➝

[For the optimal viewing experience, it is recommended to open the prototype in the Figma application, as video playback and certain interactions may be limited or inconsistent when viewed in a web browser.]
