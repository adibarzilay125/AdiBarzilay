An Abstract Reinterpretation of “Breaking the Pig”

An abstract reinterpretation of the book cover for “Breaking the Pig” by Etgar Keret, created as a visual homage. The project reimagines the story through a more mature and reflective perspective, shifting its emotional tone and narrative focus. Through minimal and symbolic visual elements, the design captures themes of desire, restraint, and transformation, offering a renewed reading of the story beyond its original context.
