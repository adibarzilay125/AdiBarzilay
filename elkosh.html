Elkosh
Title sequence for a mock documentary centered on a fictional murder incident set in the 1970s in the moshav of Elkosh.

Title sequence for a mock documentary about a fictional murder set in 1970s Elkosh. The piece combines archival footage with original material, creating a dialogue between past and presen.

Through editing, pacing, and atmosphere, it explores how documentary language shapes narrative and perception. This is a fictional project and does not refer to a real event.
