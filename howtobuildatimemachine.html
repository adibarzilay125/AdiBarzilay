How To Build A Time Machine
A short film structured as a five-step guide to building a time machine.

The film follows an eccentric young man who isolates himself in his room while attempting to discover the formula for constructing his own time machine.

As the steps unfold, the film explores themes of curiosity, solitude, and creative obsession, leading toward the climactic moment in which the machine is finally activated. The project combines narrative storytelling with a playful, pseudo-instructional format, examining the tension between imagination, invention, and the desire to transcend time.
